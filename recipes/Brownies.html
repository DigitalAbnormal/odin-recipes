Homemade Brownies

Are you craving something Soft and Sweet? Something Chewy? Something Chocolatey?

Well look no further! These delicious brownies will fulfill your cravings! They also won't take up too much of your time to make!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of White Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons of Water
  • 1 1/2 of Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 2/3 Cup of All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Baking Soda

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 325 Degrees F (165 degrees C) and Grease an 8-inch Pan
  2. Combine Sugar, Butter, and Water in a Medium Saucepan. Cook and Stir over medium heat until boiling
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth
  4. Mix in the Eggs and Vanilla
  5. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda
  6. Stir into the chocolate mixture
  7. Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan
  8. Bake the brownie batter in the preheated oven for about 20-30 minutes, or until the top is dry and the edges pull away from the sides of the pan
  9. Let Brownies cool then cut into squares
  10. Enjoy!
