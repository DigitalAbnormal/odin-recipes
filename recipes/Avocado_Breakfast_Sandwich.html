Avocado Breakfast Sandwich

Do you like Eggs? What about Avocado?

What if I told you, you could have them BOTH for Breakfast?

That's Right! I'm gonna hook you up with a BANGER Meal that will make your co-workers jealous when they see you whip this bad boy out during the morning meeting!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 2 large Eggs
  • Salt and Black Ground Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of SOFTENED Butter
  • 2 slices of Bread
  • 1 ounce of Swiss Cheese (sliced)
  • 1 ounce of Cheddar Cheese (sliced)
  • 1/2 of ripe avocado (sliced)

Directions

  1. Heat Olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Crack Eggs into skillet; then cook for 1 to 2 minutes; Flip egg and cook for 2 to 5 minutes or until done
  3. Transfer eggs onto place and season with salt and pepper
  4. Spread butter onto one side of each bread slice
  5. Lay Bread slices on the heated skillet, butter side down
  6. Quickly add Swiss Cheese, 1 fried egg, Cheddar Cheese, 1 fried egg, avocado, and the other bread slice butter side up
  7. Carefully Flip sandwich to grill other side until golden brown for 3 to 4 minutes
  8. Remove sandwich from skillet, then plate and cut sandwich in half
  9. Enjoy!
